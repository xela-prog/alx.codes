About

Young developer, serious taste.

I'm Pierre-Alexandre Merge Fignole, a computer science student but also a full-stack developer building under alx.codes. I care about websites that feel intentional: fast, responsive, readable, and memorable.

I like developer tools, Linux workflows, terminal aesthetics, and interfaces that look calm but still have personality. This portfolio is built to show that I can create for both technical people and clients who just want something that works beautifully.

Whether I'm debugging a complex component or designing a high-contrast interface, my goal is to bridge the gap between technical complexity and intentional user experience. I believe technology should feel like an extension of the user, not an obstacle; by stripping away the noise, I build digital environments that are as purposeful as they are performant. If you're looking for someone who understands both the logic under the hood and the impact on the screen, let's build something together.
